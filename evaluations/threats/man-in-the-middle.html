Threat - Man-in-the-Middle: The connection is at risk of Man-in-the-Middle (MITM) attacks. This is because LE Legacy pairing with Just Works sets the Temporary key to 0, which then is used to calculate the Short-term-key. To bolster both confidentiality and integrity, it is advised to upgrade to LE Secure Connections or employ Out-of-Band (OOB) data for secure key exchange.

This connection may also be vulnerable to identity tracking. Ensure the utilization of random and rotating BLE addresses and facilitate the exchange of an Identity Key during phase 3 of the pairing process.
