Pairing mechanism - LE Legacy Pairing: The pairing mechanism employed will be LE Legacy. This is due to either one or both devices not activating the SC Flag under Authentication Requirements within the pairing request or response packet. LE Legacy offers no protection against passive eavesdropping and minimal protection against Man-In-The-Middle (MITM) attacks. Its security is only notably more enhanced when Out-of-Band (OOB) data is utilized. For better security, consider enabling the SC Flag on your custom device if possible to use LE Secure Connections.
