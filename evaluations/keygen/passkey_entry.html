Key generation method - Passkey Entry: The selected key generation method is 'Passkey Entry'. With this method, users are required to manually enter a Passkey that is displayed on one device into the other device. In LE Legacy connections, this Passkey facilitates the exchange of a Short-Term Key. However, as the Passkey typically consists of only six digits, it is vulnerable to brute-force attacks. In the context of LE Secure Connections, the Passkey is utilized to authenticate the other device, followed by the exchange of a Long-Term Key through a public key exchange mechanism.
