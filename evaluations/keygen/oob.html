Key generation method - Out-of-band (OOB): The selected key generation method is 'Out-of-Band' (OOB). This approach involves the devices acquiring their Temporary Key in LE Legacy, or their random and confirmation values in LE Secure Connections, through an alternate medium. This medium could be NFC, QR codes, WiFi, or any other channel.

The usability of this method is highly contingent on the medium selected, and it can also provide a robust level of security. The security effectiveness of the OOB method is directly related to the security of the chosen out-of-band medium.
